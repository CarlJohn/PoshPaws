Welcome to the Best Cat Hotel the Eastern Cape has to offer!



Posh Paws is situated in Port Elizabeth on a smallholding in Theescombe. The cattery was opened in May 2007.

We, the owners, Tracy and Johnny, came up with the idea in 2006 when two catteries in Port Elizabeth closed. We then decided to build the Best Cat Hotel in Port Elizabeth. Building the cattery took a long time as we had to ensure the cats are as comfortable as they could possible be considering they in a strange place and probably a little confused. A lot of time was spent on ensuring the cattery was completely "escape proof". Our main priority is that the cats are eating, safe, healthy, happy and going the loo.
