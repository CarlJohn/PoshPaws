About Us



SOCIAL AREAS

Each kennel can accommodate up to three guests from one family. There are 61 kennels, 9 with paved gardens & 52 with grass gardens.

Each kennel has a bedroom off the garden. The bedrooms come equipped with 3 bunks, blankets, pillows, scratch post, infra-red heating lamps for those cold winter nights and a hot water bottle for those feeling a little arthritic.

The bedrooms also have a PVC doorway, which we lower in the evenings to keep the wind & chill out, but still allow for our guests to choose between being inside or out.

There are benches in the garden area for our guests to sit outside on those lovely sunny days and watch the friendly wagtails come and bath in the fountains or just to have an afternoon nap. We also supply toys for the "young one's"

Cats that are sociable, are allowed into the "Social Areas" to mingle and play. Should your cat/cats not be sociable, they will be allowed out on their own for a run around.

Our social areas are split into three sections. The one at the top includes the office and the one at the bottom includes the kitchen. You will often find our guests lying in the office or in the kitchen in the sun.

Emerald having fun with her toys.

Our Menu consists of:
Royal Canin:     Active Mature, Fit 32, Exigent 35/30, Sensible & Kitten
Iams:              Adult chicken & Kitten
Hills:               Adult chicken, Mature, Hairball control & Kitten

Should your cat/cats have a "special" diet, or you would like then to have special treats, we ask that you bring it with you.

Our guests water, food & litter trays are changed twice a day.

Medication: Should your cat/cats require medication, we will gladly administer as per your vets instructions

Should you wish to make a booking for December, it would be advisable that you book early. As we DO NOT share kennels, we fill up very quickly for December/ January holidays.
