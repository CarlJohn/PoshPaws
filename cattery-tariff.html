The Purrrrfect Holiday Home for Your Loved Ones.


TARIFFS


Our fees are R65 per night per cat.
Should your cat/cats stay longer than 30 nights, a discount will be considered.

Should frontline not be supplied for cat/cats staying more than 1 month, Posh Paws will supply at a cost of R50 per cat per month.


REQUIREMENTS (COMPULSORY)


Annual vaccinations & rabies must be up to date at least 14 days prior to arrival at Posh Paws. Please present your booklets on arrival.

De-worming & Frontline (or Revolution) must be up to date prior to arrival at Posh Paws.

Pets arriving with fleas or ticks will be Frontlined on arrival at owners expense.
